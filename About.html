About Us

At Pasta Pizza, our passion is centered on serving the authentic essence of Italian cuisine through the delightful simplicity of pizza and pasta. Nestled in the heart of OTU, our restaurant is a welcoming haven where we celebrate the time-honored art of crafting these beloved Italian classics. Our commitment to quality and tradition is evident in every dish we create, and we invite you to savor the flavors of Italy in every bite. Join us for a culinary journey that transports your taste buds to the vibrant streets of Italy, where the rich aromas and bold flavors of pizza and pasta tell a story that's both delicious and timeless.
